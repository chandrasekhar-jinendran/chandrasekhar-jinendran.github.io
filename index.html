01 / About

I'm Chandrasekhar Jinendran, a software developer currently at Bosch Global Software working on hydraulic-circuit controllers — feasibility studies, system architecture, prototype modeling, and HIL validation on STM32 hardware.

My background sits at the intersection of controls, embedded systems, and functional safety: requirement implementation through cascade/master/release branches, AUTOSAR integration, variant coding with EEPROM bit/byte configuration for FOTA, and end-to-end ownership through reviews, planning, and delivery.

I'm now extending this foundation into aerospace flight-control systems — translating ISO 26262 muscle into DO-178C / ARP4754A workflows and shipping aerospace-relevant side work in public.
